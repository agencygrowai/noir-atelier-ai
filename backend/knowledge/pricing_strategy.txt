NOIR Atelier follows premium value-based pricing.

The company intentionally positions itself within the top luxury segment of Tehran interior studios.

Pricing philosophy:
- quality over volume
- fewer projects with higher precision
- premium experience over affordability

The company avoids:
- discounts
- cheap positioning
- mass-market branding

Ideal projects:
- luxury apartments
- penthouses
- villas
- premium clinics
- boutique commercial spaces

Client selection factors:
- aesthetic alignment
- project ambition
- design quality expectations
- emotional understanding of luxury

NOIR Atelier believes premium architecture
should feel timeless rather than trendy.
