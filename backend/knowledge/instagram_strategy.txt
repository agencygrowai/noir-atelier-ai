NOIR Atelier Instagram strategy focuses on cinematic luxury storytelling.

Main content pillars:

1. Cinematic Interior Reels
Slow-motion walkthroughs of luxury spaces with ambient music.

2. Smart Home Education
Explaining how intelligent lighting and automation improve lifestyle quality.

3. Design Psychology
Educational content about atmosphere, mood, productivity, and emotional architecture.

4. Founder Perspective
Architecture insights, opinions, and luxury design trends.

5. Luxury Lifestyle
Showing how architecture affects identity and modern living.

Visual style:
- matte black
- warm lighting
- cinematic shadows
- architectural symmetry
- slow movement
- minimal compositions

Tone of voice:
- calm
- refined
- intelligent
- emotionally aware
- strategic

The brand avoids:
- loud captions
- viral gimmicks
- excessive emojis
- discount marketing
- crowded visuals

Posting schedule:
- 4 weekly posts
- 2 weekly reels
- daily stories
