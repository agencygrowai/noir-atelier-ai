NOIR Atelier is a luxury interior architecture
and smart-home integration studio based in north Tehran.

The company specializes in:
- luxury apartment interiors
- penthouse transformations
- smart-home systems
- premium lighting experiences
- modern architectural aesthetics

Brand personality:
- elegant
- minimal
- intelligent
- calm
- architectural
- emotionally refined

Brand philosophy:
"Technology should disappear into beauty."

NOIR Atelier believes luxury is not about decoration.
It is about emotional experience, atmosphere, and intelligent living.

The brand avoids:
- loud marketing
- discount positioning
- crowded visuals
- aggressive sales language

The company positions itself as a premium strategic design studio,
not a traditional decoration company.
